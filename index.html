Who we are

This club allows students of all engineering disciplines to design and work on biomedical projects. Our goal is to work with industry professionals in order to gain real world experience while applying the knowledge learned in class. This club is primarily project focused, with occasional meetings with professionals. We will be entering into various competitions throughout the year.
